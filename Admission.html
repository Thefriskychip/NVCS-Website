New Vistas Christian School - Admission

Admission Policy

1. Proof of normal or above normal intelligence. (WISC or Detroit Verbal Opposite Sub Test)

2. Proof of working below expected grade level. (Achievement Test)

3. Proof of absence of a primary emotional or behavioral problem. (Psychological testing may be required)

4. Agreement by the family and the student to the Christian teachings, principles, and discipline as outlined in the statement of faith and discipline policy. Because all of our students have academic learning difficulties, we expect to find the accompanying problems of low self-image, poor social adjustments, and emotional frustrations. The school and home must work consistently together to help the student achieve progress in all these areas

5. Proof of appropriate inoculations and citizenship.

6.Birth Certificate

New Vistas Christian School admits students of any race, color, national and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school . It does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in administration of its educational policies, admissions' policies, scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and other school administered programs.
