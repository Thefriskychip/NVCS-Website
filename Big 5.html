New Vistas Christian School - The Big 5

The Big Five Rules

(The big five rules are school wide for all classes and assemblies)

1.RESPECT GOD-Be respectful toward prayer, Bible reading, and Bible studies. Do not use God's name as a swear word, as well as unthinking use of God in speech.

2.RESPECT AUTHORITY-Be kind and respectful to the Administrator, teachers, office staff and any other parties in authority.

3.BE KIND-Be kind and respectful to peers and their rights and choices. Physical contact, name-calling, yelling and /or fighting are forbidden.

4.RESPECT PROPERTY-Respect school property and other people's property. Property defaced or destroyed must be repaired or replaced at the student's family's expense. Stealing or borrowing without permission is forbidden.

5.APPROPRIATE USE OF WORDS-Slang or vulgar words that would not glorify God, or portray good Christian values are forbidden. Students should not use words for which they do not know the meaning. Students will be held accountable for their words.
