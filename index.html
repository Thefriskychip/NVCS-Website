About New Vistas Christian School

New Vistas Christian School is located in beautiful Martinez, California. It was founded in 1978 by Bonnie Reynold after a successful summer Program. We are a private Christian school dedicated to educating the bright students who are not experiencing academic success from 5th through 12th grade. Utilizing multi-sensory teaching methods, increasing the studentâ€™s self esteem and giving them Biblical standards to live by has enriched the lives of hundreds of students who have attended New Vistas. We use Slingerland Approach which is a supportive program that encourages academic competence, effective work habits and self-discipline. Students gain self-confidence and enthusiasm for learning as they experience success in school.

Some of the highlights at New Vistas are:

* Multisensory teaching methods * Low student teacher ratios * Small class sizes * Routine testing * Life skills * Computer classes * Art Classes * Music * Positive reinforcement for discipline * Field trips * Counseling * Structured classrooms and recesses * Loving and caring staff

New Vistas is in its 34th year of educating bright kids with learning problems. Our graduates are attending Junior colleges and State Universities, Trade Schools, and/or are gainfully employed. Other students have been successfully mainstreamed into conventional classrooms at public and private schools.
