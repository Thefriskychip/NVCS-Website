New Vistas Christian School - Mission Statement

Mission Statement

NVCS is incorporated as a nonprofit religious organization for the purpose of providing a Christian school for bright students who need special instructional methods because of a specific learning difficulty.

Our goal:

"To Provide a Christian based, multi-sensory, structured learning environment to those with average or above average intelligence who do not experience academic success in conventional classrooms and to build them up spiritually, emotionally, and intellectually; enabling them to meet their full potential as a child of God."
